POLLUTION Made Simple

Plastic pollution is the accumulation of plastic objects and particles on the earth's environment through toxic pollutants. It can take hundreds or even thousands of years for plastics to decay or break down so the environmental damage is long lasting. It is affects all the organism in the food chain from tiny species like a plankton to wales. So the next time you visit a super market, say no to a plastic bag! Your small act of saying "No to a plastic" may say a life! Let's be aware and benevolent!
