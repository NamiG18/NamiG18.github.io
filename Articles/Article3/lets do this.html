Let's Do This!

Plastic is basically harmful to us because it's not degradable. Can we live without it? Not entirely, but we can surely reduce the damage it causes.
In Bhutan, I feel that one of the main causes of waste on the street is due to shortage of stable waste disposal places. If we could install dustbins of good quality like in other countries we can have a clean city.

We can also practice BYOB which stands for Bring Your Own Bags which will greatly reduce the source of waste.

Next time you're in the market, don't ask for plastic bags.
